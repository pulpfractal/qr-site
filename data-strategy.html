The national forecasting system has begun to drift. Over the past 18 months, model accuracy has declined across multiple sectors. Internal review suggests the issue is not computational — but informational. Two independent data strategy teams have been identified as holding the missing variables required to stabilise the system: Team Alpha — Market Behaviour Unit Team Beta — Consumer Signals Division Both teams have been instructed to prepare a submission to the central model. However, due to prior incidents involving data misuse and attribution disputes, a new safeguard has been introduced: Submissions will be processed simultaneously. Teams will not see the other’s decision until after execution. Each team must choose one of the following: FULL DISCLOSURE Provide all relevant datasets, including proprietary signals and derived features. LIMITED RELEASE Provide only baseline data, retaining key variables internally.
